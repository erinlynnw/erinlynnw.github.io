Lily Allen Music Website

My favorite part of this website is the home page. simplistic yet grand, all it contains is the artist's logo and a search bar, with ornate branded decorations and a button to buy the music. I just love how the brick wall you're apparently looking at from the outside has shadows that move across it, giving the appearance of actually looking at a wall shaded by trees. small movement (GIFS) on a homescreen is a really interesting idea I'd like to use.
