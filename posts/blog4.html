I love how unique the navigation is for this website, as well as how everything kind of hits you at once while you look at it. Since cartoons aren't really my thing I would love to see how this looks with photographs, as my portfolio homepage. This grabbed my attention right away and I wanted to keep playing with it and seeing more. I was afraid that it would be easy to get lost with such an obscure navigation component but when you leave the home page the next page reboots, changes color so you know you're on another page, and an X appears that takes you back to the homepage when you're finished with your selection. For added efficiency there is also an expandable menu bar on the sub-pages.
